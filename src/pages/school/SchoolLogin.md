## Logging in

<br />

**Purpose:** To access the Luminate system fully, a login form is
      prompted depending on the selected method of log in, i.e. Student Login,
      School Login and Content Creator Login.

<br />
<br />

### How to login  as an existing User

<br />
<br/>

![alt text](/images/Log.png "Title")


* Click on the  **Login** button at the top right corner of the navbar
* They can also access the login form by clicking on  [this link](https://www.luminate.ng/LoginView).

<br />

![alt text](/images/SchooLogin.png "Title")

<br />

* Click on **Login** button to direct you to School login form

* Enter your Email address
* Enter your Password
* Click on the **login** button to direct you to the school page
